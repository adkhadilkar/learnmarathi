Core outcomes

Measure success across reading, writing, and motivation.

Reading

  • Recognize स्वर/व्यंजन, conjuncts, मात्रा.
  • Decode new words beyond memorization.
  • Read sentences, paragraphs, and stories.

Writing

  • Correct stroke order and letter shapes.
  • Write बाराखडी combinations accurately.
  • Spell by sound + pattern, not copying.

Motivation

  • Daily 5–10 minute habit with streaks.
  • Confidence-first feedback loops.
  • Parent-approved rewards and limits.
