Marathi Literacy App (Ages 4–10)

Deep, progressive Marathi reading & writing in 5–10 minutes a day.

A combined curriculum, lesson architecture, and content system that takes kids from अक्षर to कथा with engaging practice, feedback, and healthy rewards.

Daily Mission

  • Warm-up: अक्षर टॅप करा
  • Teach: स्वर/मात्रा ऑडिओ
  • Practice: 2 मिनी-गेम्स
  • Apply: शब्द व वाक्य वाचन
  • Reward: कथा अनलॉक

Core outcomes

Measure success across reading, writing, and motivation.

Reading

  • Recognize स्वर/व्यंजन, conjuncts, मात्रा.
  • Decode new words beyond memorization.
  • Read sentences, paragraphs, and stories.

Writing

  • Correct stroke order and letter shapes.
  • Write बाराखडी combinations accurately.
  • Spell by sound + pattern, not copying.

Motivation

  • Daily 5–10 minute habit with streaks.
  • Confidence-first feedback loops.
  • Parent-approved rewards and limits.
