# Curriculum Map

## Level 0: Pre-literacy
- Directionality, matching shapes, visual discrimination.
- Listening games: identify first sound/syllable.
- Picture vocabulary with spoken audio.

## Level 1: अक्षर ओळख (Letter recognition)
- स्वर (vowels) and व्यंजन (consonants).
- Letter names + sounds with visual examples.
- Confusion sets (e.g., क/ख, त/थ, ट/ठ).

## Level 2: मात्रा + बाराखडी
- Vowel signs (मात्रा) with consonants.
- Short vs long vowel distinctions.
- Real words, not isolated grids.

## Level 3: Word decoding + spelling patterns
- Common syllable patterns and word families.
- Conjuncts and अर्धाक्षर.
- Nasalization (ं/ँ) and visarga (ः) when relevant.

## Level 4: Sentence reading + comprehension
- Simple sentence patterns and punctuation.
- Picture-choice to text-choice comprehension.

## Level 5: Story reading + writing
- Graded readers with tap-to-hear.
- Guided writing prompts.

## Level 6 (Optional): Grammar & style
- लिंग/वचन basics, postpositions, verb forms.
- Synonyms, antonyms, idioms, proverbs.

## Starter units
- Unit A: स्वर (13) + recognition + tracing.
- Unit B: व्यंजन groups (क-वर्ग, च-वर्ग…) + minimal pairs.
- Unit C: core मात्रास (ा ि ी ु ू े ै ो ौ) with selected consonants.
- Unit D: top conjuncts (start with 20 most common).
