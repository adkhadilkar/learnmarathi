# मराठी शिकूया — Marathi Shikuya 🐿️

> A friendly, interactive website that teaches you to **read and write Marathi** —
> made for people who already *speak* the language but never learned the script.

**▶️ Live demo: https://adkhadilkar.github.io/learnmarathi/**

## Why this exists

Millions of people grow up *speaking* Marathi at home but never learn to **read or
write** it — the script (देवनागरी) was never taught, or was taught as joyless drills.

Marathi Shikuya flips that around. You already know thousands of Marathi words by
ear. This site connects those sounds you *know* to the letters you *don't* — one
friendly letter at a time, with **real Marathi audio**, familiar example words, and
a giant-squirrel mascot named **Shekru** (शेकरू) cheering you on. No grammar
lectures, no boring drills.

It's designed to be approachable for **kids and adults alike**.

## What's inside

| Section | What you learn |
| --- | --- |
| 🎯 **Learn** | A *hear → see → quiz* flow for every vowel and consonant, with a reward animation when you get it right. |
| 🔤 **Letters** | All **15 vowels (स्वर)** and **36 consonants (व्यंजन)**, each with a picture and an everyday example word. |
| 📝 **Barakhadi** | Every consonant combined with all 12 vowel signs — **372 syllables** — as flashcards or a reference table. |
| 🔗 **Jodakshara** | Conjunct (joined) letters: how two consonants merge, and how they sound. |
| 🔢 **Numbers** | Marathi numerals **०–२०** with their spoken names. |
| 🦚 **Animals** | Beautiful photo flashcards — tap to hear the animal's name in Marathi. |
| 📚 **Stories** | Illustrated storybooks read page-by-page, with audio and word-by-word highlighting, plus a sticker when you finish. |

Everything is paired with **native-Marathi audio** so you learn by ear and eye
together. There's a **light/dark mode**, it works great on **phones and desktops**,
and there may be a hidden surprise or two if you poke around. 🌰

## How to use it

1. Open the **[live site](https://adkhadilkar.github.io/learnmarathi/)**.
2. Tap **“Start with vowels”** — you'll *hear* a letter, *see* it next to a word you
   already say, then a quick tap-quiz locks it in.
3. Tap **any letter, syllable, number, animal, or sentence** anywhere on the site to
   hear it spoken aloud.
4. Move on to **Barakhadi** and **Jodakshara** once the basic letters feel familiar —
   together they unlock most everyday Marathi text.
5. Finish with the **Stories** to put it all together. 🎉
6. Tap the **logo** any time to return home (and tap it again *on* the home page for
   a little something extra).

No account, no sign-up, and **no personal data is collected**.

## Audio

Pronunciation uses a **native Marathi voice**, so sounds unique to Marathi — like
`च`, `ज`, and `ळ`, and the retroflex/dental pairs (`ट` vs `त`) — are spoken
correctly. Clips are bundled with the site, so audio works even on a flaky
connection.
